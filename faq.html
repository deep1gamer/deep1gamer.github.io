Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I get server access?

Each completed ad gives you 24 hours of access to your selected server.

Why do I need to watch ads?

Ad revenue helps us maintain free servers. We use HilltopAds for ethical monetization.

Can I use an ad blocker?

No. Ad blockers will prevent server access. We only show non-intrusive ads.
